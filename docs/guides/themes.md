# Change the theme

Snakie comes in two looks: a bright **Light** theme and a cosy **Dark** theme. This page shows you how to switch between them.

## Where the theme picker lives

The theme setting is in the **Settings** window, on the **Appearance** tab.

1. Look at the activity bar — the tall strip of icons down the left edge of Snakie.
2. Click the **gear** icon (Settings) near the bottom.
3. In the window that opens, click the **Appearance** tab.
4. Under **Theme**, click **Light** or **Dark**.

That's it! The whole app changes straight away, and your choice is saved automatically — Snakie remembers it next time you open it.

## The two themes

| Theme | What it looks like |
| --- | --- |
| **Light** | The bright default. A *skeuomorph* skin — meaning it copies real materials like brushed metal, felt, and cream notebook paper. |
| **Dark** | A calm, lights-out look that's easy on the eyes in a dark room. |

!!! tip
    Both themes work everywhere in Snakie — the editor, the shell, the Board View and all the instruments. Pick whichever feels nicest to you; you can change it back any time.

!!! note
    On the same **Appearance** tab you'll also find a **Breadboard background** choice (Dark or Blueprint) for the Board View. That's separate from the app theme.
